About Us

In an era of rapid change, where environmental concerns and economic realities are increasingly intertwined, Econology emerges as a platform dedicated to critical analysis, informed discourse, and change. This monthly magazine is the collective vision of students, research enthusiasts, aspiring data scientists, and journalists with backgrounds in applied economics and other social sciences.United by our shared passion for insightful writing and rigorous analysis, we seek to explore the complex intersections of ecology, environmental and behavioral economics.
Our mission is clear: to provide readers with in-depth perspectives that go beyond mainstream narratives. While many publications address economic trends or environmental issues in isolation, we believe these spheres are inseparable.Economic policies shape our environment, just as ecological realities redefine our economic futures. The challenge lies in striking a balance acknowledging the urgency of environmental sustainability while recognizing the multifaceted forces that drive global and national economies.Bangladesh, like many developing nations, faces pressing environmental and economic dilemmas. Climate finance negotiations, sustainable development, and resource management are no longer abstract concepts but pivotal concerns that will determine the country’s future.
Through well-researched write-ups, opinion pieces, and analysis, Econology aims to shed light on these critical issues, advocating for policies that priorities both ecological, environmental responsibility and economic growth.Beyond climate and sustainability, we will explore geopolitics, business and finance, art Shakil Khan (Editor-in-Chief) and culture, science and technology, and the philosophical trends shaping the 21st century. Our goal is to challenge conventional perspectives, engaging with the world as it is complex, nuanced, and often uncomfortable.We extend our heartfelt gratitude to our contributors, readers, and all those who have supported us in bringing this first issue to life. Econology is more than a magazine; it is a commitment to intellectual integrity, thoughtful discourse, and the relentless pursuit of knowledge.
As we embark on this journey, we invite you to join us in shaping a platform that not only informs but also inspires us.
In God, we trust for success.
